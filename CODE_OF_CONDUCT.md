# ExecuNova AI Code of Conduct

ExecuNova AI is an open, inclusive, and professional community. All contributors must follow these guidelines:

## Our Standards

- Be respectful, courteous, and professional  
- No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination  
- Be patient and collaborative when reviewing or receiving feedback  
- Credit original ideas and sources

## Reporting Issues

If you witness any violation:  
- Contact project maintainers via email: `ankitkumarforall@gmail.com`  
- Include a clear description of the incident and any relevant screenshots  
- All reports are confidential and will be addressed promptly

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove content, block contributors, or take other actions to maintain a safe, inclusive environment.

Together, we create a supportive environment where everyone can innovate and succeed.
