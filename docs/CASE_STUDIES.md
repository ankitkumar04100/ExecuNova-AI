# ExecuNova AI — Case Studies

## Scenario 1: Student Assignment

**Problem:**  
- Student has a 20-hour assignment due in 3 days  
- Plans 7 hours per day → high risk (80%)

**ExecuNova Solution:**  
- Splits tasks into structured subtasks  
- Allocates realistic hours per day  
- Predicts risk → drops to 25%  
- Student completes on time  

---

## Scenario 2: Developer Sprint

**Problem:**  
- Developer has 40-hour sprint in 5 days → unrealistic  
- Overload causes missed deadlines

**ExecuNova Solution:**  
- Scheduler reallocates tasks across 7 days  
- Predicts and mitigates bottlenecks  
- Risk reduced → 20%  

---

## Scenario 3: Product Launch

**Problem:**  
- Multiple overlapping deadlines → high stress  
- Impossible to track progress manually

**ExecuNova Solution:**  
- Dynamic AI detects overload days  
- Generates optimized daily plans  
- Alerts for potential delays  
- Risk reduced → 15%

## Scenario 4: Daily Workload Optimization

**Problem:**  
- User energy levels fluctuate  
- High chance of burnout and inefficiency  

**ExecuNova Solution:**  
- Assigns subtasks based on energy levels  
- Detects early bottlenecks  
- Ensures balanced workload  
- Improves productivity without fatigue
